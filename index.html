Accessible Slider Scale Demo

I’ve worked in research for a few years and used several survey-building tools. One thing I kept running into was how difficult it is to make surveys truly accessible — and even when they claim to be accessible, the experience for screen reader users is often poor.

This is a simple attempt at a clean, accessible slider-based question.

Demo

0 means “Naw, it's not for me.” and 9 means “I'll club penguins for a bar!”
