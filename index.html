Accessible Slider Scale Demo

I'm legally blind and I’ve worked in research for a few years and used several survey-building tools. I kept running into challenges like making surveys truly accessible, and even when they claim to be accessible, the experience for screen reader users is often poor. It was insane that I needed sighted assistance to fill "accessible" forms!

This is a simple, clean, accessible slider-based question.

Demo

0 = Not interested.
9 = Extremely interested.
